<p><p>Electronics AGV market can be categorized into three main types based on their applications. Unit Load Type AGVs are designed to transport individual unit loads such as pallets or containers within a warehouse or manufacturing facility. Automated Forklift Type AGVs are equipped with forks to lift and move heavy loads, mimicking the functionality of a traditional forklift. Tugger Type AGVs are designed to tow carts or trailers filled with goods, making them ideal for material handling in a variety of industries.</p></p>
