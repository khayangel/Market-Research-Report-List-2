<p><strong>The Marine Inertial Navigation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Precision</li><li>Normal Precision</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine inertial navigation systems can be classified into two main types: high precision and normal precision. High precision systems provide extremely accurate positioning and navigation data, making them ideal for applications that require precision such as military, scientific research, and commercial industries. Normal precision systems, on the other hand, offer adequate accuracy for many standard marine navigation needs, such as commercial shipping and recreational boating. Both types of systems utilize inertial sensors to continuously track the position, orientation, and velocity of a vessel at sea.</p></p>
