<p><p>Intensive Care Monitors come in two main types: Desktop Monitors and Portable Monitors. Desktop Monitors are typically larger and stationary, designed for use at a patient's bedside in a hospital setting. They provide a comprehensive range of monitoring parameters and have larger screens for easy viewing by medical professionals. Portable Monitors, on the other hand, are smaller and lightweight, allowing for easy transport between different locations within a healthcare facility or even for use in ambulances. They offer essential monitoring functions in a more compact and mobile design.</p></p>
