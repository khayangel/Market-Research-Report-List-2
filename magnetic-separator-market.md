<p><p>The magnetic separator market is segmented into three types: Tubular Magnetic Separator, Square Magnetic Separator, and Roller Magnetic Separator. Tubular Magnetic Separators are cylindrical in shape and are most commonly used for removing fine ferrous particles from a product stream. Square Magnetic Separators have a square shape and are suitable for materials with a high density. Roller Magnetic Separators consist of several rollers that rotate and attract magnetic materials, making them ideal for separating large quantities of magnetic materials efficiently.</p></p>
