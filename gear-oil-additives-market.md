<p><p>Gear Oil Additives Market Types are essential for improving the performance and longevity of gear oils. Antioxidants prevent oxidation and extend the oil's lifespan. Friction Reducers help improve efficiency and smooth operation. Corrosion Inhibitors and Rust Inhibitors prevent metal degradation and rust formation. Metal Deactivators remove traces of metals to prevent catalytic reactions and extend the oil's life. These additives play a crucial role in maintaining gear oil quality and ensuring proper lubrication.</p></p>
