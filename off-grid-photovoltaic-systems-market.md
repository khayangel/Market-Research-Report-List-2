<p><p>Off-grid photovoltaic systems are standalone solar power systems that are not connected to the grid. These systems typically include solar panels, batteries for energy storage, charge controllers, inverters, and sometimes a generator for backup power. Off-grid photovoltaic systems are typically used in remote areas where there is no access to the main power grid, or in locations where connecting to the grid is not economically feasible.</p><p>The Off-grid Photovoltaic Systems Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.8% during the forecast period.
