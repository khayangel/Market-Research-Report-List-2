Sensor Rotary instruments utilize sensors to measure roundness and cylindrical profiles of objects by rotating them. Worktable Rotary instruments, on the other hand, have a fixed sensor and a rotating worktable that moves the object to be measured. Both types of instruments are essential for industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace to ensure the accuracy and quality of cylindrical components.</p></p>
