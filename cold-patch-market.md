<p><ul><li>Semi-solid</li><li>Solid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cold patch is a type of asphalt patching material that is used to quickly and temporarily repair potholes and other pavement damage. It is available in two main market types: semi-solid and solid. Semi-solid cold patch is a mix of asphalt, aggregate, and a petroleum-based binder, which remains pliable at cold temperatures. Solid cold patch, on the other hand, is a pre-mixed material that does not require heating before application. Both types provide quick and cost-effective solutions for repairing pavement damage.</p></p>
