<p><ul><li>Dry Spinning Method</li><li>Wet Spinning Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chlorine resistant fiber market includes two main types: dry spinning method and wet spinning method. Dry spinning method involves dissolving the polymer in a volatile solvent and spinning it into fibers through a spinneret. Wet spinning method includes dissolving the polymer in a non-volatile solvent and extruding it into a liquid bath to form fibers. Both methods result in chlorine resistant fibers that are suitable for applications where exposure to chlorine is common, such as swimwear and sportswear.</p></p>
