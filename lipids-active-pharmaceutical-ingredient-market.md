<p><p>Lipids Active Pharmaceutical Ingredient (API) Market can be categorized into three main types based on their structure and composition: Simple Lipids, Compound Lipids, and Derived Lipids. Simple Lipids are basic fats and oils, Compound Lipids contain additional elements like phosphorus or carbohydrates, and Derived Lipids are formed from the breakdown of complex lipids. Each type of lipid API plays a unique role in pharmaceutical formulations and can have different pharmacokinetic properties, making them suitable for various drug delivery systems.</p></p>
