<p><p>Daylight fluorescent pigments are categorized into three types - thermoset, thermoplastic, and other (such as aqueous dispersions). Thermoset type pigments are heat-resistant and durable, making them suitable for applications requiring high temperatures. Thermoplastic type pigments are more versatile and can be easily molded and reshaped. Other types like aqueous dispersions offer water-based solutions for specific applications. Each type of daylight fluorescent pigment caters to different needs in various industries, ensuring a wide range of options for manufacturers and consumers.</p></p>
