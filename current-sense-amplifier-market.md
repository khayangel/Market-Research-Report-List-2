<p><strong>The Current Sense Amplifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-side Sensing</li><li>Low-side Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Current sense amplifiers are used to measure and amplify small signals to provide accurate current measurements. In high-side sensing, the current sense amplifier is placed on the high side of the load, allowing measurement of current flowing from the power supply to the load. In low-side sensing, the current sense amplifier is placed on the low side of the load, measuring the return current flowing back to the power supply. Both types of current sensing have their own advantages and applications depending on the specific requirements of the system.</p></p>
