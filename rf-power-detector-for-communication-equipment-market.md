<p><p>RF power detectors are commonly used in communication equipment to measure the power of radio frequency signals. Two popular types of RF power detectors are logarithmic detectors and RMS detectors. </p><p>Logarithmic detectors provide an output signal that is proportional to the logarithm of the input power level, making them ideal for applications where a wide dynamic range is required. </p><p>RMS detectors, on the other hand, provide an output signal that is proportional to the RMS value of the input signal, making them suitable for applications where accurate power measurements are needed. Each type of detector has its own unique advantages and is used in different communication equipment markets.</p></p>
