<p><strong>The Handmade Soap Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CP (Cold Process)</li><li>HP (Hot Process)</li><li>MP (Melt & Pour)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handmade soap market includes various types such as CP (Cold Process), HP (Hot Process), and MP (Melt & Pour). CP soaps are made by mixing oils and lye, while HP soaps involve cooking the ingredients to speed up the saponification process. MP soaps are made by melting pre-made soap bases and adding colorants and fragrances. Each type appeals to different consumer preferences, with CP soap known for its natural ingredients, HP for its rustic appearance, and MP for its ease of customization.</p></p>
