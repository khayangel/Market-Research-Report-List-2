<p><strong>The Tri-band Wireless Router Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>802.11a</li><li>802.11b</li><li>802.11g</li><li>802.11n</li><li>802.11ac</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tri-band wireless routers operate on three different frequency bands simultaneously, providing faster and more stable internet connections. The 802.11a, 802.11b, and 802.11g standards operate on the 2.4GHz frequency band, while the 802.11n standard operates on both the 2.4GHz and 5GHz bands. The latest standard, 802.11ac, operates exclusively on the 5GHz band, offering even faster speeds and greater bandwidth. Each market type offers varying levels of performance and compatibility with different devices.</p></p>
