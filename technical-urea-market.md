<p><p>Technical urea is classified into three different market types based on the particle sizes. Small particles (0.85mm-2.8mm) are commonly used in industries where a quick release of nitrogen is required. Medium particles (1.18mm-3.35mm) are preferred for slow-release applications such as golf courses and landscaping. Large particles (2mm-4.75mm) are mostly used in agricultural fields for direct soil application. Each size has specific applications based on the speed of nitrogen release and the intended use in various industries.</p></p>
