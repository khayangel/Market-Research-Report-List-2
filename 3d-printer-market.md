<p><ul><li>FDM（Fused Deposition Model）</li><li>SLA（Stereolithography）</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling) 3D printer market is characterized by its affordability and versatility, making it popular among hobbyists and small businesses. On the other hand, the SLA (Stereolithography) 3D printer market targets professionals and industries requiring high precision and detailed prints. SLA printers utilize a laser to cure liquid resin into solid objects, offering superior detail and surface finish compared to FDM printers. Both markets cater to different user needs and preferences in the rapidly growing 3D printing industry.</p></p>
