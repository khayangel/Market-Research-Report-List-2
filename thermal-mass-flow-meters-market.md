<p><p>Thermal mass flow meters are used in various industries for measuring the flow rate of gases. Industrial thermal mass flow meters are specifically designed for harsh industrial environments and can handle high temperature and pressure conditions. Capillary-tube type thermal mass flow meters utilize a capillary tube to measure flow rates accurately. Other types of thermal mass flow meters include insertion thermal mass flow meters, which are used for large ducts or pipes, and inline thermal mass flow meters, which are installed directly in the pipeline for continuous monitoring.</p></p>
