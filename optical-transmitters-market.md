<p><p>Optical transmitters are used in high-speed data transmission applications. Common types in the market include SFP, SFP+, QSFP/QSFP+, XFP, and CXP transmitters. Small Form-factor Pluggable (SFP) modules are compact and cost-effective. SFP+ offers higher data rates than SFP. Quad Small Form-factor Pluggable (QSFP/QSFP+) transceivers allow for high-density and high-speed connections. XFP transceivers are used for 10Gbps applications. CXP transceivers provide high-speed links for data centers and networking equipment. Each type of optical transmitter caters to different data transmission needs in various industries.</p></p>
