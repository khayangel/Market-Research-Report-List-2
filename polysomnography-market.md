<p><p>Polysomnography (PSG) is a test that records sleep patterns and various bodily functions during sleep. There are three main types of PSG markets: electroencephalography (EEG), which measures brain activity during sleep; dynamic PSG, which records movements and muscle activity; and video PSG, which captures visual images of the individual during sleep. Each type provides valuable information for diagnosing sleep disorders and guiding treatment. EEG PSG focuses on brain activity, dynamic PSG on movements, and video PSG on visual observations during sleep.</p></p>
