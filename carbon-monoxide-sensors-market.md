<p><p>Carbon Monoxide Sensors in the market are available in two main types: Semiconductor and Infrared. Semiconductor sensors utilize a chemical reaction to detect carbon monoxide levels, while Infrared sensors work by measuring the absorption of infrared light by carbon monoxide molecules. Semiconductor sensors are typically more affordable and commonly used in residential settings, while Infrared sensors are known for their high accuracy and are often used in industrial and commercial applications where precision is crucial.</p></p>
