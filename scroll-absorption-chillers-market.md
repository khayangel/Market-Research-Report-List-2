<p><p>Scroll chillers utilize a scroll compressor, a type of positive-displacement compressor, to cool air or water for various applications. These chillers are highly efficient and have low maintenance needs, making them popular in commercial and industrial settings. Absorption chillers, on the other hand, use a heat source, usually steam or hot water, to generate cooling through an absorption process. They are often used in applications where waste heat is readily available, such as in industrial processes or district cooling systems.</p></p>
