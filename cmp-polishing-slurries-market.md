<p><strong>The CMP Polishing Slurries Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alumina Slurry</li><li>Colloidal Silica Slurry</li><li>Ceria Slurries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CMP polishing slurries are used in the semiconductor industry for planarization and polishing of surfaces. There are three main types of slurries commonly used: alumina slurry, colloidal silica slurry, and ceria slurries. Alumina slurries are abrasive and used for metals and hard materials, colloidal silica slurries are non-abrasive and used for softer materials, while ceria slurries are used for high-precision polishing. Each type of slurry offers unique properties and benefits for specific polishing applications in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
