Coal Tar Pitch is a dark, viscous material derived from coal tar which is mainly used in roofing applications. Asphalt, on the other hand, is a sticky, black and highly viscous liquid or semi-solid form of petroleum that is widely used in road construction. Natural Asphalt, also known as bitumen, is a naturally occurring substance found in certain rock formations and is commonly used in the production of waterproofing materials and road surfacing.</p></p>
