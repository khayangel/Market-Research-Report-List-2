<p><p>Automobile Plate Spring Assembly consists of two main types - Multi-Leaf Spring and Little-Leaf Spring. Multi-Leaf Springs are made up of multiple leaves stacked on top of each other, providing more stability and load-bearing capacity. They are commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles like trucks and buses. On the other hand, Little-Leaf Springs have fewer leaves and are more flexible, providing a smoother ride quality. They are often used in light vehicles like cars and SUVs. Both types play a crucial role in determining the suspension system's performance and overall ride comfort of the vehicle.</p></p>
