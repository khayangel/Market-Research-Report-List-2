Two major types of gas sensors in the market include Non-Dispersive Infrared (NDIR) sensors and Chemical sensors. NDIR sensors operate based on the absorption of infrared light by gases, while Chemical sensors use chemical reactions to detect and quantify gases. Both types of sensors are widely used in applications such as environmental monitoring, automotive, healthcare, and industrial safety.</p></p>
