<p><p>Tufted carpets come in two main types: machine-tufted carpets and hand-tufted carpets. Machine-tufted carpets are manufactured using automated machinery, resulting in a more uniform and cost-effective product. On the other hand, hand-tufted carpets are crafted by skilled artisans using a handheld tufting gun, giving them a more unique and artisanal touch. Both types cater to different market segments, with machine-tufted carpets being more popular in mass-produced settings, while hand-tufted carpets are favored for their high-quality craftsmanship and individuality.</p></p>
