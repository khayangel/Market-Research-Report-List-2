<p><ul><li>Monolayer Stainless Steel Honeycomb Core</li><li>Multilayer Stainless Steel Honeycomb Core</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stainless Steel Honeycomb Core is available in two main types, Monolayer and Multilayer. Monolayer honeycomb cores consist of a single layer of stainless steel hexagonal cells, offering lightweight strength and stiffness. Multilayer honeycomb cores, on the other hand, are constructed with multiple layers of stainless steel hexagonal cells bonded together, providing enhanced mechanical properties and durability. Both types are commonly used in applications such as aerospace, marine, and architectural industries for their excellent strength-to-weight ratio and corrosion resistance.</p></p>
