<p><p>Automatic Boom Barrier System Market Types include Hydraulic Barriers and Electromechanical Barriers. Hydraulic Barriers operate using a hydraulic mechanism to lift and lower the boom arm, providing high security and reliability. Electromechanical Barriers, on the other hand, use an electric motor to operate the barrier, making them more energy-efficient and quicker to open and close. Both types of barriers are widely used in parking lots, toll booths, and other restricted access areas to control vehicle entry and exit.</p></p>
