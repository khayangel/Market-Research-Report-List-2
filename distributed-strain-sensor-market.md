<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Distributed Strain Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Based on the Rayleigh Backscatter Deliver</li><li>Based on Fiber Bragg Gratings (FBGs) Deliver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distributed Strain Sensors based on Rayleigh Backscatter deliver real-time strain information by analyzing the backscatter of light along the fiber optic cable. This type of sensor is typically used for large-scale infrastructure monitoring. On the other hand, Distributed Strain Sensors based on Fiber Bragg Gratings (FBGs) use periodic changes in the refractive index to detect strain. These sensors are known for their accuracy and durability and are commonly used in aerospace, civil engineering, and oil & gas industries.</p></p>
