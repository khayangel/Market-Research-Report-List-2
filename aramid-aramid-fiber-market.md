<p><p>Aramid fibers are high-performance synthetic fibers known for their strength, heat resistance, and chemical resistance. There are two main types of aramid fibers - para-aramid fibers and meta-aramid fibers. </p><p>Para-aramid fibers, such as Kevlar, are known for their exceptional strength and are commonly used in ballistic protection and industrial applications. </p><p>Meta-aramid fibers, such as Nomex, are valued for their heat resistance and are often used in flame-retardant clothing and thermal insulation.</p><p>Both types of aramid fibers play a crucial role in various industries due to their excellent properties.</p></p>
