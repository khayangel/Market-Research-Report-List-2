<p><p>Resting ECG devices are used to monitor the electrical activity of the heart at rest, providing valuable information on the heart's function. Stress ECG devices are used during exercise to evaluate the heart's response to physical activity. Other types of ECG telemetry equipment may include Holter monitors for continuous heart monitoring, event monitors for intermittent monitoring, and mobile cardiac telemetry for real-time remote monitoring. These different types of equipment offer clinicians a variety of options for monitoring and diagnosing heart conditions.</p></p>
