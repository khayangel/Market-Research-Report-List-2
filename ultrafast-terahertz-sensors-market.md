<p><p>Ultrafast Terahertz Sensors are utilized in two main markets: Photonic Sensing and Electronic Sensing. Photonic Sensing involves the use of light to detect and analyze terahertz signals, making it suitable for applications where high sensitivity and accuracy are critical. On the other hand, Electronic Sensing relies on electronic components to detect terahertz signals, allowing for faster response times and lower costs compared to photonic sensing. Both markets cater to different needs and requirements in various industries such as healthcare, security, and telecommunications.</p></p>
