<p><strong>The Ceramic Additive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dispersant</li><li>Lubricant</li><li>Grinding Aid</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic additives are categorized into different types based on their functions. Dispersants are used to improve the dispersion of particles in a ceramic mixture, ensuring uniformity. Lubricants help reduce friction during processing and improve the workability of the ceramic material. Grinding aids assist in the grinding process, enhancing efficiency and reducing energy consumption. Other types of ceramic additives may include binders, plasticizers, and deflocculants, each serving a unique purpose in enhancing the performance and properties of ceramic products.</p></p>
