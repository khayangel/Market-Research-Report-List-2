<p><p>Hybrid cloth diapers are available in two main types - single-layer and multi-layer. On the other hand, multi-layer diapers have multiple absorbent layers that can be customized for different absorbency levels. Single-layer diapers are preferred for their trimness and ease of use, while multi-layer diapers are popular for their versatility and ability to adjust absorbency based on the baby's needs.</p></p>
