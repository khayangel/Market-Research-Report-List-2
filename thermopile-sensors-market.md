<p><p>Thermopile sensors are available in two types: thermopile infrared sensors and thermopile laser sensors. Thermopile infrared sensors measure infrared radiation and convert it into temperature readings, making them ideal for applications like temperature control and monitoring. Thermopile laser sensors, on the other hand, are used to detect laser radiation, making them essential for applications such as laser alignment and distance measurement. Both types of thermopile sensors play a crucial role in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and healthcare.</p></p>
