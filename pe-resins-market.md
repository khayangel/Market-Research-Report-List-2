<p><p>PE resins market includes three main types: HDPE, LDPE, and LLDPE. HDPE, or high-density polyethylene, is known for its high strength-to-density ratio and is widely used in various industries such as packaging, construction, and automotive. LDPE, or low-density polyethylene, is flexible and transparent, making it suitable for applications like plastic bags and wraps. LLDPE, or linear low-density polyethylene, offers enhanced flexibility and puncture resistance, making it ideal for packaging films and industrial applications.</p></p>
