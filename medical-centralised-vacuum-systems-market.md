<p><p>Medical Centralised Vacuum Systems are utilized in healthcare settings to remove potentially hazardous waste and maintain a clean environment. There are two main types in the market - Cyclonic Central Vacuum Systems and Filtered Central Vacuum Systems. Cyclonic systems use centrifugal force to separate debris from the airflow, whereas filtered systems use various types of filters to remove particles. Both systems offer efficient and reliable waste removal solutions for medical facilities, ensuring a safe and hygienic environment for patients and staff.</p></p>
