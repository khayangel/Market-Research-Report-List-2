<p><p>Hard Coated (HC) Films come in two types - Type I and Type II. Type I HC Films are known for their scratch resistance, making them ideal for applications where durability is key, such as in electronic device screens. On the other hand, Type II HC Films offer both scratch resistance and anti-glare properties, making them suitable for outdoor displays and automotive applications.
