<p><p>Thin Layer Chromatography Systems market includes three main types: Classical TLC, High Performance TLC (HPTLC), and Preparative TLC (PLC). Classical TLC is the traditional method using glass plates for separation and identification of compounds. HPTLC uses advanced technology, such as automated systems and high-resolution plates, for improved separation and analysis. PLC is used for large-scale isolation and purification of compounds. Each type caters to different needs and requirements in various industries, such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverages, and environmental testing.</p></p>
