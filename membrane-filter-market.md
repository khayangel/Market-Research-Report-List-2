<p><p>Membrane filters are of various types including inorganic membrane filters, PVDF filters, PTFE filters, PES filters, and others. Inorganic membrane filters are composed of materials like ceramic or metal for higher chemical resistance. PVDF filters are known for their excellent chemical compatibility and low protein binding properties. PTFE filters are hydrophobic and resistant to chemicals and high temperatures. PES filters are known for their low protein binding and high flow rates. Other membrane filters may include nylon, cellulose acetate, or mixed cellulose ester filters.</p></p>
