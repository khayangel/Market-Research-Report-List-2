<p><p>A self-aligning ball bearing is a type of bearing that can accommodate misalignment of the shaft relative to the housing. This type of bearing is designed with two rows of balls and a common concave spherical raceway in the outer ring, giving it the ability to self-align. This feature allows the bearing to compensate for shaft misalignment, reducing friction and extending the bearing's lifespan.</p><p>The self-aligning ball bearing market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.3% during the forecast period. One of the key drivers of market growth is the increasing demand for these bearings in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and industrial machinery. The ability of self-aligning ball bearings to operate efficiently even when misaligned makes them ideal for applications where shaft deflection or misalignment is common.</p><p>Another trend driving the growth of the self-aligning ball bearing market is the increasing adoption of advanced technologies in bearing manufacturing, leading to improved performance and durability of these bearings.
