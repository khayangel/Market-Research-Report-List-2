<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Motion Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Proximity Detection</li><li>Ranging Measurement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic motion sensors are classified into two main market types: Proximity Detection and Ranging Measurement. Proximity Detection sensors are mainly used for detecting the presence of objects or individuals within a specific range, triggering actions such as turning on lights or opening doors. Ranging Measurement sensors, on the other hand, provide precise distance measurements by calculating the time taken for ultrasound waves to travel to an object and back. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in various industries for automation, security, and safety purposes.</p></p>
