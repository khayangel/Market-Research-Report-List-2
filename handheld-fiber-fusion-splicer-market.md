<p><strong>The Handheld Fiber Fusion Splicer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cladding Alignment</li><li>Core Alignment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld Fiber Fusion Splicers come in two main types: Cladding Alignment and Core Alignment. Cladding Alignment splicers align the outer cladding of the fibers before fusing them together, making them ideal for single-mode fibers. Core Alignment splicers align the fiber cores, ensuring minimal signal loss and high performance, making them suitable for both single-mode and multi-mode fibers. Both types of splicers are essential tools for technicians working in the telecommunications industry to create reliable and efficient fiber optic connections.</p></p>
