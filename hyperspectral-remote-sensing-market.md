<p><strong>The Hyperspectral Remote Sensing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>VNIR</li><li>SWIR</li><li>Thermal LWIR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hyperspectral remote sensing refers to the process of collecting and analyzing information about an object or area through a wide range of wavelengths. This technology is divided into three main market types based on the specific wavelengths used: VNIR (Visible and Near-Infrared), SWIR (Short-Wave Infrared), and Thermal LWIR (Long-Wave Infrared). VNIR sensors are suitable for analyzing surface features, SWIR sensors are effective for mineral identification, while Thermal LWIR sensors are used for temperature mapping and detecting heat signatures. Each market type serves different purposes in various industries.</p></p>
