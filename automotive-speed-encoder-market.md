<p><strong>The Automotive Speed Encoder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Encoder</li><li>Radial Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Speed Encoders are categorized into two main types: Axial Encoders and Radial Encoders. Axial Encoders are designed to measure linear displacement along the axis of rotation, providing accurate speed measurements. On the other hand, Radial Encoders are known for measuring rotational speed and position, making them suitable for applications where precise radial movement needs to be monitored. Both types of encoders play a crucial role in the automotive industry by helping vehicles operate efficiently and safely.</p></p>
