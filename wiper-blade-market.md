<p><p>There are three main types of wiper blades in the market: Boneless Wiper Blades, Bone Wiper Blades, and Hybrid Wiper Blades. Boneless wiper blades do not have a metal frame, offering a sleek design and improved performance. Bone wiper blades have a traditional metal frame and are durable and cost-effective. Hybrid wiper blades combine the features of both boneless and bone wiper blades, providing a balance between performance and durability. Each type caters to different preferences and needs of consumers in the market.</p></p>
