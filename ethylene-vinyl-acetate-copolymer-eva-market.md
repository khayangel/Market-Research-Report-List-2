<p><p>Ethylene Vinyl Acetate Copolymer (EVA) is categorized into three main types based on the vinyl acetate (VA) content. EVA with VA content up to 4% is commonly used in packaging materials and hot melt adhesives. EVA with VA content ranging from approximately 4 to 30% is used in applications like shoe soles and sports equipment due to its flexibility and durability. EVA with VA content greater than 40% is utilized in industries such as photovoltaic modules and wire & cable insulation due to its excellent thermal properties.</p></p>
