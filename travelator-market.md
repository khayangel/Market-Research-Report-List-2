<p><p>Travelator markets can be categorized into three main types - parallel, multi-parallel, and others. In parallel markets, multiple travelators run in the same direction side by side, allowing for efficient movement of large crowds. Multi-parallel markets feature several rows of travelators moving in the same direction, providing even more capacity for travelers. Other markets may include a mix of different configurations, such as intersecting travelators or circular layouts. Each type of market offers unique advantages for optimizing passenger flow and enhancing the overall travel experience.</p></p>
