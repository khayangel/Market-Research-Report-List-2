<p><p>Removable adhesives are commonly divided into two main types: transparent and opaque. Transparent removable adhesives are clear and allow for visibility through the adhesive layer, making them ideal for applications where the underlying surface needs to remain visible. On the other hand, opaque removable adhesives are not transparent and provide more coverage, making them suitable for situations where complete concealment of the surface is desired. Both types offer temporary bonding properties and are easily removable without leaving residue behind, making them versatile options for various applications.</p></p>
