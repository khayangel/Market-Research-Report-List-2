<p><p>Emulsified asphalt can be classified into two main types: anionic emulsified asphalt and cationic emulsified asphalt. Anionic emulsified asphalt is negatively charged, while cationic emulsified asphalt is positively charged. These types are differentiated based on their chemical properties and how they interact with aggregates and water. Apart from these two main types, there are also other specialized emulsified asphalt products available in the market that cater to specific applications and requirements in the construction industry.</p></p>
