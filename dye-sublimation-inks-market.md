<p><p>Dye sublimation inks are categorized into two main types: water-based dye sublimation ink and oil-based sublimation ink. Water-based dye sublimation ink is known for its eco-friendliness and ability to produce vibrant colors, making it a popular choice for printing on textiles and other materials. On the other hand, oil-based sublimation ink is preferred for its durability and resistance to fading, making it suitable for outdoor applications and products that require long-lasting prints. Both types of inks have their own advantages and are used in various industries based on their specific characteristics.</p></p>
