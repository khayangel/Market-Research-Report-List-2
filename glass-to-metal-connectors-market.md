<p><strong>The Glass-to-Metal Connectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Matched Connectors/Seals</li><li>Compression Connectors/Seals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glass-to-Metal Connectors Market Types include Matched Connectors/Seals and Compression Connectors/Seals. Matched connectors/seals are designed to have similar coefficients of thermal expansion between the glass and metal, ensuring a tight and reliable seal. Compression connectors/seals use pressure to create a secure connection between the glass and metal components. Both types of connectors are crucial for applications requiring hermetic seals, such as in aerospace, automotive, and electronics industries.</p></p>
