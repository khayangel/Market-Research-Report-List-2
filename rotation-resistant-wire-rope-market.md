<p><strong>The Rotation Resistant Wire Rope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Square Section</li><li>Hexagonal Cross Section</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rotation resistant wire ropes are commonly found in two market types: square section and hexagonal cross section. Square section wire ropes feature a design that reduces rotation and enhances stability during lifting operations. On the other hand, hexagonal cross section wire ropes offer increased resistance to twisting and spinning, making them ideal for applications where rotation must be kept to a minimum. Both types of wire ropes are utilized in various industries such as construction, mining, and offshore drilling for their durability and reliability.</p></p>
