<p><p>Natural Hydraulic Lime (NHL) is classified into three main types based on their strength and properties: NHL2, NHL3.5, and NHL5. NHL2 has the lowest strength and is suitable for internal plastering and masonry work. NHL3.5 is ideal for general building applications and offers a balanced strength level. NHL5 is the highest strength type, used for heavy-duty construction projects like bridges and retaining walls. Each type of NHL is carefully formulated to meet specific construction requirements, offering a range of options for various building needs.</p></p>
