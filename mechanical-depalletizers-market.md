<p><p>Mechanical depalletizers are commonly divided into two main types: Conventional depalletizers and Robotic depalletizers. Conventional depalletizers are traditional machines that use mechanical arms or conveyors to remove products from pallets. These machines are typically lower in cost but require more manual intervention. On the other hand, Robotic depalletizers use advanced robotic technology to automatically unload products from pallets. While they may have a higher initial cost, they offer increased efficiency and flexibility in handling various types of products.</p></p>
