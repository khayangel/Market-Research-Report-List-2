<p><strong>The ECMO First Aid Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>VA (Veno-Arterial) ECMO</li><li>VV (Veno-Venous) ECMO</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ECMO first aid equipment market comprises two main types: VA (Veno-Arterial) and VV (Veno-Venous) ECMO systems. VA ECMO provides both cardiac and respiratory support by oxygenating and removing carbon dioxide from the blood, while VV ECMO only assists with respiratory function by oxygenating the blood. Both types of ECMO devices are essential in providing life-saving support to patients with severe cardiopulmonary conditions, and are crucial tools in critical care and emergency medicine settings.</p></p>
