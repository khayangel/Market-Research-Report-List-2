<p><p>Fingerprint Powders are used in forensic investigations to reveal hidden fingerprints on surfaces. There are three main types of fingerprint powders in the market: Conventional, Magnetic, and Fluorescent. Conventional powders consist of a colorant and base, while Magnetic powders contain iron shavings along with a colorant. Fluorescent powders utilize a light-stimulated colorant and base. Each type of powder has its own advantages and uses, catering to different needs in forensic analysis and fingerprint identification.</p></p>
