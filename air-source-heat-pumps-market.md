<p><strong>The Air Source Heat Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-to-Air</li><li>Air-to-Water</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air Source Heat Pumps come in two main types - Air-to-Air and Air-to-Water. Air-to-Air heat pumps extract heat from the outside air and distribute it within the building through a network of air ducts. This type is commonly used for space heating and cooling. On the other hand, Air-to-Water heat pumps transfer heat from the outside air to a water-based central heating system. This type is typically used for providing hot water and heating for radiators or underfloor heating.</p></p>
