<p><p>PCB Membrane Switches are widely used in various industries due to their durability and cost-effectiveness. There are two main types of materials used in PCB Membrane Switches: Polyester (PET) and Polycarbonate (PC). PET is known for its flexibility and resistance to abrasion, making it suitable for applications requiring frequent use. On the other hand, PC offers higher durability and is more resistant to chemicals and extreme temperatures, making it ideal for harsher environments. Both materials offer reliable performance and are popular choices in the PCB Membrane Switch market.</p></p>
