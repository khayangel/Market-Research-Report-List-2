<p><strong>The Rail Transit Traction Converter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Propulsion Converter</li><li>Integrated Compact Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rail transit traction converters are crucial components in powering electric trains. There are two main types of traction converters: propulsion converters and integrated compact converters. Propulsion converters are specifically designed to drive the traction motors of the train, providing the necessary power and control for acceleration and deceleration. Integrated compact converters, on the other hand, combine multiple functions such as power conversion, energy storage, and traction control in a single compact unit, saving space and improving efficiency in rail transit systems.</p></p>
