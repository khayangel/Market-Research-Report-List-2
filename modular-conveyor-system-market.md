<p><p>Modular conveyor systems can be classified into different types based on their design and functionality. Roller conveyors utilize rollers to transport items along a path, while belt conveyors use a continuous belt to move goods. Overhead conveyors are suspended from the ceiling and are suitable for transporting items across different levels. Pallet conveyors are used to transport pallets of goods efficiently. Other types of modular conveyor systems may include slat conveyors, chain conveyors, and screw conveyors, each designed to meet specific material handling needs.</p></p>
