<p><p>Hollow Fiber Membrane Market consists of three main types: Microfiltration, Ultrafiltration, and Nanofiltration. </p><p>Microfiltration membranes have larger pore sizes to separate suspended solids and bacteria. Ultrafiltration membranes have smaller pore sizes to remove viruses, proteins, and macromolecules. Nanofiltration membranes have even smaller pore sizes to separate ions and smaller organic molecules. Each type of membrane serves a specific purpose in water treatment, pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and other industries requiring efficient separation processes.</p></p>
