<p><strong>The Smoked Atlantic Salmon Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Smoking</li><li>Cold Smoking</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smoked Atlantic salmon is traditionally prepared using two main methods: hot smoking and cold smoking. In hot smoking, the fish is cooked at a higher temperature (usually around 120-180°F) and has a flakier texture with a more intense smoky flavor. Cold smoking, on the other hand, is done at a much lower temperature (usually around 70-90°F) and results in a silkier texture with a milder smoky taste. Both types of smoked salmon offer distinct flavors and textures, catering to different preferences in the market.</p></p>
