<p><p>Trunk piston engine oil is categorized into three main types based on the speed of the engine they are designed for: low speed engine oil, middle speed engine oil, and high speed engine oil. Low speed engine oil is formulated for engines with low rpm, typically used in marine vessels and stationary power plants. Middle speed engine oil is designed for engines with moderate rpm, commonly found in industrial applications. High speed engine oil is tailored for engines with high rpm, such as those used in automotive and aviation industries.</p></p>
