<p><p>The market for bar code readers can be divided into two main types: handheld barcode scanners and stationary barcode scanners. Handheld barcode scanners are portable devices that are used to scan barcodes by being physically passed over the code. Stationary barcode scanners, on the other hand, are fixed in place and can automatically scan barcodes as they pass by the scanner. Both types of scanners are commonly used in retail, inventory management, and logistics industries to track and manage products efficiently.</p></p>
