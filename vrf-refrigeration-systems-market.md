<p><p>VRF Refrigeration Systems come in two main types: Cooling Mode and Reversible Mode. Cooling Mode systems are designed to provide cooling to indoor spaces by extracting heat from the air and transferring it outside. On the other hand, Reversible Mode systems can switch between cooling and heating functions, making them versatile and suitable for both summer and winter months. These systems are highly efficient and cost-effective, making them popular choices for commercial and residential applications.</p></p>
