<p><p>Auto Grade LiDAR scanners are divided into two main types: Mechanical LiDAR and Solid State LiDAR. </p><p>Mechanical LiDAR scanners use rotating mirrors or other moving parts to scan and capture the surroundings. They are more common in the market due to their proven technology and cost-effectiveness.</p><p>On the other hand, Solid State LiDAR scanners use solid-state components such as semiconductors for scanning. They are preferred for their reliability, compactness, and lower power consumption, making them suitable for various automotive applications.</p></p>
