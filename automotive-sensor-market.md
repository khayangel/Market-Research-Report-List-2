<p><p>Automotive sensor market can be divided into various types including Temperature Sensors, Position Sensors, Inertial Sensors, Pressure Sensors, and Speed Sensors. Temperature Sensors monitor the temperature of the engine, coolant, and oil to prevent overheating. Position Sensors determine the position of various components like throttle position or crankshaft. Inertial Sensors measure acceleration and rotation of the vehicle. Pressure Sensors monitor the pressure within the engine or braking system. Speed Sensors track the speed of the vehicle for various functions like ABS or cruise control.</p></p>
