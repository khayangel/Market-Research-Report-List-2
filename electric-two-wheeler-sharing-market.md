<p><p>Electric two-wheeler sharing markets can be categorized into dockless and dock-based systems. Dockless systems do not require designated parking stations, allowing users to pick up and drop off vehicles anywhere within a specified operating area. On the other hand, dock-based systems require users to pick up and return vehicles to designated parking stations. Dockless systems offer more flexibility and convenience for users, while dock-based systems provide a structured and organized approach to vehicle management. Both types have their own advantages and cater to different user preferences.</p></p>
