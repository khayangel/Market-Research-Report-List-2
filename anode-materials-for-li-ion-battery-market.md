<p><p>Anode materials for Li-ion battery market consists of three main types: artificial graphite, natural graphite, and silicon-based anode. Artificial graphite is manufactured through high-temperature treatment of carbon-based materials, providing high stability and conductivity. Natural graphite is sourced from graphite ore and offers good performance at a lower cost. Silicon-based anode materials have high energy density but are prone to expansion during charging, leading to reduced cycle life. Each type of anode material offers different benefits and trade-offs for Li-ion battery applications.</p></p>
