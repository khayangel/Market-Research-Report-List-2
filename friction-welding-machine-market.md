<p><p>Friction welding machines are used in various industries for joining materials. There are three main types of friction welding machines: rotary friction welding machine, linear friction welding machine, and friction stir welding machine. </p><p>Rotary friction welding machines use rotational motion to create friction between materials for joining. Linear friction welding machines use linear motion to achieve the same result. Friction stir welding machines, on the other hand, use a non-consumable tool to join materials by stirring them together. Each type of machine has its unique benefits and applications in the market.</p></p>
