<p><ul><li>Laser</li><li>Structure Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Infrared thermography market types include laser and structure light technologies. Laser thermography utilizes laser beams to measure temperature by detecting the intensity of the infrared radiation emitted by an object. Structure light thermography uses a grid or pattern of light to create a 3D scan of the object, allowing for accurate temperature measurements. Both technologies are commonly used in industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, and automotive for applications such as predictive maintenance, quality control, and temperature monitoring.</p></p>
