<p><strong>The Titanium Forging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impression Die</li><li>Open Die</li><li>Rolled Ring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Titanium forging market can be categorized into three main types: Impression Die, Open Die, and Rolled Ring forging. Impression Die forging involves shaping metal using a closed die under high pressure. Open Die forging uses two flat dies to manipulate the metal into the desired shape. Rolled Ring forging utilizes circular dies to form seamless rings from metal. Each type of forging method offers unique advantages and applications in the manufacturing industry, depending on the specific needs of the product being produced.</p></p>
