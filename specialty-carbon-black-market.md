<p><p>Specialty Carbon Black market is segmented into various types including Lamp Black, Acetylene Black, Gas Black, and Others. Lamp Black is a type of carbon black produced by burning oil or natural gas. Acetylene Black is produced through the thermal decomposition of acetylene gas. Gas Black is produced by the incomplete combustion of natural gas. The "Others" category includes carbon blacks produced by various other methods. Each type has unique characteristics and applications in industries such as paints, inks, and plastics.</p></p>
