<p><p>Corrugated High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) Pipe is segmented into two main types: Single-Wall and Double-Wall. Single-Wall corrugated HDPE Pipe is used for drainage and non-pressure applications, offering flexibility and easy installation. On the other hand, Double-Wall corrugated HDPE Pipe provides added strength and protection, making it suitable for high-pressure applications such as sewage and stormwater management. Both types of pipes are commonly used in various construction and infrastructure projects due to their durability, versatility, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
