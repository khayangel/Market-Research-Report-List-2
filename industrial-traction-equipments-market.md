<p><strong>The Industrial Traction Equipments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traction Transformer</li><li>Traction Motor</li><li>Traction Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial traction equipments market includes traction transformer, traction motor, and traction converter. Traction transformer is responsible for adjusting the voltage of power supply for efficient operation of traction motors. Traction motors convert electrical power into mechanical power to drive the vehicles. Traction converters control the flow of electrical energy between the power source and the traction motor, ensuring smooth and efficient operation. Together, these components form the backbone of industrial traction systems, powering various transportation vehicles and equipment.</p></p>
