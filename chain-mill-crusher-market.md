<p><p>Chain mill crusher market can be categorized into single axis and double axises based on the number and direction of the rotating shafts. Single axis chain mill crushers consist of one horizontal shaft with a series of chain links while double axises have two parallel shafts with chain links. Single axis crushers are suitable for smaller scale operations, while double axis crushers are more efficient for high volume crushing. Both types offer effective size reduction and processing of materials in various industries.</p></p>
