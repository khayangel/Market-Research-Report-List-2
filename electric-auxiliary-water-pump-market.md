<p><p>Electric Auxiliary Water Pump market is divided into two types based on voltage requirements: 12V EWP and 24V EWP. 12V EWP is designed for vehicles that operate on a 12-volt electrical system, such as most passenger cars and light trucks. On the other hand, 24V EWP is more suitable for heavy-duty vehicles like trucks and buses that run on a 24-volt electrical system. Both types of water pumps serve the same purpose of circulating coolant to regulate engine temperature, but differ in voltage compatibility.</p></p>
