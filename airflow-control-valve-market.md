<p><p>Airflow control valves can be categorized into two main types based on their design and operation: Exiting Cylinder and Entering Cylinder valves. Exiting Cylinder valves control the airflow exiting a system or component, while Entering Cylinder valves regulate the airflow entering a system or component. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining optimal airflow and pressure levels within various applications, ensuring efficient and effective operation of pneumatic and hydraulic systems. Each type offers specific benefits and functionalities to suit different operational requirements.</p></p>
