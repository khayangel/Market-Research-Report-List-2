<p><ul><li>Ordinary Rubber</li><li>Modified Rubber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Natural rubber flooring comes in two main types: Ordinary rubber and Modified rubber. Ordinary rubber is made from pure rubber material without any additional modifications, making it more cost-effective but less durable. On the other hand, Modified rubber is enhanced with additives like fillers, pigments, or binders to improve its properties such as durability, slip-resistance, and appearance. This results in a higher quality and longer-lasting flooring option, albeit at a higher cost. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
