<p><p>Halogen-free flame retardant nylon market includes different types such as Red Phosphorus, Melamine Salts, and Others. Red Phosphorus is an effective flame retardant that releases phosphoric acid when exposed to heat, forming a protective layer on the material's surface. Melamine salts work by releasing nitrogen gas when heated, which dilutes oxygen and inhibits combustion. Other types include additives like metal hydroxides and intumescent materials that expand when exposed to fire, forming a protective barrier. These options provide versatile solutions for enhancing the fire resistance of nylon materials.</p></p>
