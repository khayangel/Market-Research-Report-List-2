<p><p>Industrial ceramics market can be categorized into different types based on the materials used in their manufacturing process. The major types include Alumina, Mullite, Cordierite, and Others. Alumina ceramics are widely used for their excellent mechanical and electrical properties. Mullite ceramics are known for their high-temperature resistance and thermal shock resistance. Cordierite ceramics are commonly used in catalytic converters and kiln furniture due to their low thermal expansion. The "Others" category includes various ceramic materials that are used for specialized applications in different industries.</p></p>
