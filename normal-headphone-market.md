On-ear headphones have smaller ear cups that rest on the ears, offering a balanced blend of sound quality and portability. In-ear headphones, also known as earbuds, are small and fit directly into the ear canal, making them highly portable and great for on-the-go use. Each type caters to different preferences and needs of consumers.</p></p>
