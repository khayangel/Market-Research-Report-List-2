<p><p>MgO wallboard is categorized into three main types based on thickness: thin, medium, and thick. Thin MgO wallboards have a thickness below 8mm, making them ideal for applications where weight and space are critical considerations. Medium MgO wallboards have a thickness of 8-15mm, offering a balance between weight and strength for general construction purposes. Thick MgO wallboards have a thickness above 15mm, providing optimal durability and sound insulation for heavy-duty construction projects.</p></p>
