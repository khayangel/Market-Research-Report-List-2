<p><p>Shielded cables are classified into different types based on the type of shielding used to protect the signal from electromagnetic interference. Metal foil shielded cables use a thin layer of metallic foil to provide protection. Compile net shielded cables use a woven mesh of conductive material for shielding. Other types of shielding may include combinations of both metal foil and compile net, as well as unique materials or designs tailored to specific applications. Each type of shielding offers varying degrees of protection depending on the environment and requirements of the system.</p></p>
