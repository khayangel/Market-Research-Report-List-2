<p><ul><li>Inner Rotor Motor</li><li>Outer Rotor Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>BLDC Electric Motor Market types include Inner Rotor Motor and Outer Rotor Motor. Inner Rotor Motors have the rotor located inside the stator, providing a compact design with high efficiency and torque. On the other hand, Outer Rotor Motors have the rotor located outside the stator, offering higher power density and cooling capabilities. Both types are commonly used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and robotics, with Inner Rotor Motors being preferred for applications requiring compactness and Outer Rotor Motors for high power requirements.</p></p>
