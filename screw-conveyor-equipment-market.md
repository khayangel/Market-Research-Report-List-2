<p><strong>The Screw Conveyor Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shaft Screw Conveyor</li><li>Shaftless Screw Conveyor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Screw Conveyor Equipment Market includes two main types: Shaft Screw Conveyor and Shaftless Screw Conveyor. Shaft Screw Conveyors have a central shaft running through the length of the conveyor, providing stability and support for the screw blades. On the other hand, Shaftless Screw Conveyors do not have a central shaft, allowing for larger handling capacities and reduced clogging. Both types are widely used in industries such as agriculture, food processing, mining, and wastewater treatment for efficient material handling and transportation.</p></p>
