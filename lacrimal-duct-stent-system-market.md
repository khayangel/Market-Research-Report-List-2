<p><p>Lacrimal duct stent systems are used in ophthalmic procedures to treat conditions like blocked tear ducts. There are two main types of stents available in the market: mono canalicular stents and canalicular stents. Mono canalicular stents are inserted into one of the tear ducts, while canalicular stents are placed in both tear ducts. These stents help maintain the opening of the lacrimal ducts, allowing tears to drain properly and relieving symptoms such as excessive tearing or eye infections.</p></p>
