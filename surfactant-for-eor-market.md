<p><p>Surfactants for EOR are classified into various types based on their chemical structure and properties. Anionic sulfonate surfactants are commonly used for their ability to lower interfacial tension. Anionic carboxylate surfactants are effective at reducing oil-water interfacial tension. Nonionic surfactants are versatile and can be used in a wide range of conditions. Biosurfactants are derived from biological sources and are considered environmentally friendly. Other types of surfactants may include cationic or amphoteric surfactants, each with their own unique properties for enhancing oil recovery during EOR processes.</p></p>
