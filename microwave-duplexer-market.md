<p><p>Microwave duplexer market types include crystal type, ceramic type, and others. Crystal type duplexers utilize piezoelectric crystals to separate signals at different frequencies, offering high performance and reliability. Ceramic type duplexers use ceramic materials to achieve similar signal separation, often with a more cost-effective solution. Other types of duplexers may utilize different technologies or materials to meet specific requirements. Each type of duplexer caters to different applications and preferences within the microwave industry, providing a range of options for signal management and communication.</p></p>
