<p><p>The automotive electric window regulator market consists of various types such as scissor regulator, cable regulator, flexible shaft regulator, and others. Scissor regulators are commonly used in vehicles as they provide stable and efficient movement for the window. Cable regulators use cables to control the movement of the window. Flexible shaft regulators utilize a flexible shaft to operate the window smoothly. Other types of regulators may include rack and pinion systems or gear-driven mechanisms for window control in vehicles.</p></p>
