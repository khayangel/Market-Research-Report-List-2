<p><p>Organic linen market includes three main types of yarn: dry spun yarn, wet spun yarn, and blended yarn. Dry spun yarn is made through a chemical-free process that retains the natural properties of the linen fibers. Wet spun yarn involves a process of soaking the linen fibers before spinning them into yarn. Blended yarn is a combination of linen fibers with other natural or synthetic fibers to create unique textures and properties. Each type of yarn offers different characteristics and benefits for various applications in the organic linen market.</p></p>
