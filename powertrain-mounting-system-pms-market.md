<p><p>The Powertrain Mounting System (PMS) market types include Pure Rubber Mounting and Hydraulic Mounting. Pure Rubber Mounting systems use rubber material to absorb vibrations and noise from the powertrain, providing smooth operation and reducing wear on vehicle components. Hydraulic Mounting systems use fluid-filled chambers to provide additional damping and support. Both types offer benefits in terms of isolating the powertrain from the vehicle chassis, improving overall comfort and performance. The market for these systems continues to grow as vehicle manufacturers prioritize customer satisfaction and efficiency.</p></p>
