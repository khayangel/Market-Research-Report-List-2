<p><strong>The High Voltage Amplifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unipolar</li><li>Bipolar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Voltage Amplifier Market is divided into two main types: Unipolar and Bipolar. Unipolar amplifiers operate with a single power supply voltage, making them simpler and more cost-effective. They are commonly used in applications where precise control of positive voltage is required. On the other hand, Bipolar amplifiers use both positive and negative power supply voltages, allowing for greater flexibility and improved signal quality. They are often employed in applications where voltage swings in both directions are necessary.</p></p>
