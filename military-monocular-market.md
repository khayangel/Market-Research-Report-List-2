<p><p>The military monocular market consists of two main types: Image Intensifier and Thermal Image. Image Intensifier monoculars use technology to amplify existing light, making objects visible even in low-light conditions. On the other hand, Thermal Image monoculars detect heat signatures to provide images in complete darkness or through various weather conditions. Both types play crucial roles in military operations, with Image Intensifier monoculars being ideal for night vision and Thermal Image monoculars offering advanced detection capabilities.</p></p>
