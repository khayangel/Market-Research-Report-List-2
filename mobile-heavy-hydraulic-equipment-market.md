<p><strong>The Mobile Heavy Hydraulic Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Pump Drive</li><li>Pump-Accumulator Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct pump drive systems in mobile heavy hydraulic equipment consist of a pump directly connected to the engine, providing continuous power to operate various hydraulic functions. Pump-accumulator drive systems utilize an accumulator to store energy from the pump and then release it when needed, allowing for higher power output during peak demand. While direct pump drive systems offer simplicity and efficiency, pump-accumulator drive systems provide improved energy efficiency and reduced fuel consumption, making them suitable for applications with varying power requirements.</p></p>
