Magnetic stationary cycles use magnets to create resistance, providing a smooth and quiet workout experience. Electromagnetic cycles use an electromagnetic current to control resistance levels, allowing for precise adjustments. Self-generation cycles generate power as the user pedals, eliminating the need for batteries or cords.
