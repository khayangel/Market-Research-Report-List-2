<p><p>The Vehicle Steer-by-wire System market includes various types of components such as Electronic Control Unit, Road Wheel Actuators, Force Feedback Actuator, Steering Motor, and Angular Sensor. The Electronic Control Unit plays a key role in processing signals and controlling the system. Road Wheel Actuators are responsible for physically turning the wheels. Force Feedback Actuators provide a simulated steering feel. Steering Motors help in turning the wheels, while Angular Sensors detect the steering angle. These components work together to enable a smooth and precise steer-by-wire system in vehicles.</p></p>
