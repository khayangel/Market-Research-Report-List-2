<p><strong>The Photoelectric Profiler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Camera Style</li><li>Knife-edge</li><li>Slit</li><li>Pinhole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photoelectric profilers come in various market types such as camera style, knife-edge, slit, and pinhole. Camera style profilers use a CCD camera to capture images of the light profile. Knife-edge profilers employ a sharp blade to measure the intensity of the light passing through. Slit profilers use a narrow slit to capture light information. Pinhole profilers use a small hole to restrict the light entering the system. Each market type offers unique features and benefits for different applications in light profiling.</p></p>
