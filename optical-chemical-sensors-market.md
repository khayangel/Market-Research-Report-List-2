<p><p>Optical chemical sensors are used for detecting and measuring various chemicals and gases in different environments. The market for these sensors can be categorized into different types such as Infrared sensors, Fibre optic sensors, Photoionisation sensors, and others. Infrared sensors detect chemical compounds based on their absorption of infrared radiation, while fibre optic sensors utilize light transmission for detection. Photoionisation sensors ionize gas molecules to analyze their composition. Other types of optical chemical sensors include colorimetric sensors and surface plasmon resonance sensors.</p></p>
