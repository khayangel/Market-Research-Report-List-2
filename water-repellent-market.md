<p><p>The water repellent market is segmented into three main types: water-based, solvent-based, and silicone-based products. Solvent-based repellents provide a durable and long-lasting barrier against water, but may contain harmful chemicals. Silicone-based repellents offer superior water resistance and durability, making them ideal for high-performance applications.
