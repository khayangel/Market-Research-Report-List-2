<p><p>RF Mixers are electronic devices used in radio frequency applications to combine two or more signals into a single output. There are two main types of RF Mixers: Active Mixers and Passive Mixers. </p><p>Active Mixers require an external power source and typically have higher conversion gain and linearity compared to Passive Mixers. On the other hand, Passive Mixers do not require an external power source and are simpler in design but may have lower conversion gain and linearity. Both types play a crucial role in RF signal processing and communication systems.</p></p>
