<p><p>Fiber optic jumpers come in two main types: single-mode and multimode. Single-mode jumpers are designed for long-distance communication, utilizing a single light beam to transmit data over greater distances with high speed and low loss. Multimode jumpers, on the other hand, are suitable for shorter distances and use multiple light beams to transmit data. They are typically used in local area networks and data centers where high bandwidth is required over shorter distances.</p></p>
