<p><p>Intumescent paints are specifically designed to expand and form a protective char when exposed to high temperatures, such as in the event of a fire. This char acts as an insulating layer, slowing down the spread of flames and protecting the underlying substrate from heat damage. Intumescent paints are commonly used in buildings, especially in areas where fire protection is crucial, such as stairwells, corridors, and structural steel.</p><p>The global Intumescent Paints Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.7% during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to the increasing emphasis on fire safety regulations and building codes across the world. Additionally, the growing awareness about the importance of passive fire protection measures is driving the demand for intumescent paints in various end-user industries, including construction, oil and gas, and automotive.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the Intumescent Paints Market include the development of environmentally-friendly formulations, advancements in technology to enhance fire protection performance, and increasing investments in research and development to introduce innovative products.
