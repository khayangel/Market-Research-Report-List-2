<p><strong>The Injection Robot Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Servo Robot</li><li>Pneumatic Robot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Injection Robot market is divided into two main types: Servo Robot and Pneumatic Robot. Servo Robot market involves robots that are driven by a servo motor, providing precise control and high-speed operation. These robots are commonly used in applications requiring high levels of accuracy and repeatability, such as automotive and electronics industries. On the other hand, Pneumatic Robots use compressed air as their power source, making them cost-effective and suitable for simpler tasks like pick and place operations in industries such as packaging and food processing.</p></p>
