<p><p>Brake Boosters are essential components in vehicles that amplify the force applied to the brake pedal, making braking easier and more efficient. There are three main types of Brake Boosters - Vacuum Booster, Hydraulic Booster, and Air Pressure Booster. Vacuum Boosters utilize engine vacuum to assist in brake pedal force, Hydraulic Boosters use hydraulic pressure, and Air Pressure Boosters utilize compressed air to enhance braking power. Each type offers unique benefits and characteristics, catering to different vehicle and driving needs.</p></p>
