<p><p>Middle Voltage Circuit Breakers market consists of various types such as Miniature Circuit Breaker (MCB), Moulded Case Circuit Breaker (MCCB), and Air Circuit Breaker (ACB). MCBs are compact, used for low voltages, and have thermal and magnetic trip elements. MCCBs are larger, used for higher currents, and provide thermal and magnetic protection. ACBs are larger and used for higher voltages, providing thermal and short-circuit protection. Each type caters to different voltage and current requirements in various applications within the electrical industry.</p></p>
