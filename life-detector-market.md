<p><p>Life detector market is categorized into different types based on the technology used for detection. Radar-based systems utilize radio waves to detect movement and breathing patterns. Audio-based systems use microphones to detect sounds associated with life, such as breathing or heartbeat. Infrared-based systems detect body heat and movement. Other types of life detectors may include seismic-based systems that detect vibrations, or chemical-based systems that analyze air composition for signs of life. Each type of detector offers unique advantages and applications for detecting life in various scenarios.</p></p>
