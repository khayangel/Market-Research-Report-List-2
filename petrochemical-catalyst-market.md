<p><p>Petrochemical catalyst market types include FCC catalyst, which is used in fluid catalytic cracking to convert heavy petroleum fractions into lighter, more valuable products. Hydrotreating catalysts are used in the removal of impurities from feedstocks, while hydrocracking catalysts are used to break down heavy hydrocarbons into lighter products. Polymerization catalysts are used in the production of polymers, alkylation catalysts in the production of high-octane gasoline, and reforming catalysts in the production of high-quality gasoline blends. Other market types may include isomerization catalysts, dehydration catalysts, and desulfurization catalysts.</p></p>
