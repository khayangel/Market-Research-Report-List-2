<p><p>Heavy lifting equipment can be classified into three main types based on their operation mechanisms: manually operated, hydraulic powered, and electrical powered. Manually operated equipment requires physical strength and labor to operate, making it suitable for smaller scale lifting tasks. Hydraulic powered equipment uses fluid pressure to lift heavy loads, offering more strength and efficiency than manual equipment. Electrical powered equipment utilizes electricity to lift heavy loads, providing even more power and precision for complex lifting tasks.</p></p>
