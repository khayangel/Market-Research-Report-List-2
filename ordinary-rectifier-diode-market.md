<p><p>Single tube rectifier diodes consist of a single diode within a tube package, suitable for low power applications. Double tube rectifier diodes, on the other hand, include two diodes within a single tube package, enabling higher voltage and current handling capabilities. Single tube diodes are more commonly used in household electronics and small appliances, while double tube diodes are utilized in industrial and heavy-duty applications where higher power requirements are necessary. Both types serve the purpose of converting alternating current to direct current efficiently.</p></p>
