<p><ul><li>Polarographic Dissolved Oxygen Meters</li><li>Primary Battery Type Dissolved Oxygen Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dissolved Oxygen Meters are commonly categorized into two main types: Polarographic Dissolved Oxygen Meters and Primary Battery Type Dissolved Oxygen Meters. Polarographic meters use a sensor with a platinum cathode and an anode to measure oxygen levels in water, while Primary Battery Type meters utilize a disposable battery to power the device. Both types are widely used in various industries such as environmental monitoring, wastewater treatment, aquaculture, and scientific research to determine the oxygen levels in water bodies.</p></p>
