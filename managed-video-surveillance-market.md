<p><p>Managed Video Surveillance market includes two main types: Analog Video Surveillance System and IP Video Surveillance System. Analog systems use traditional methods of transmitting video footage through coaxial cables, while IP systems use digital technology to send and receive video data over a network. Analog systems are generally more affordable and familiar to users, while IP systems offer higher quality video and advanced features such as remote access and analytics. Both systems are used by businesses and individuals to monitor and secure their properties.</p></p>
