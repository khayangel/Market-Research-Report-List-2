<p><p>Automated Storage & Retrieval Systems (AS/RS) are used to automatically store and retrieve goods from designated storage locations. Three main types of AS/RS systems include Vertical Lift Storage Modules (VLSM), Unit Load Stackers, and ASRS Gantry Robots. VLSMs use vertical lift mechanisms to access stored goods efficiently. Unit Load Stackers handle larger loads and stack them in designated storage locations. ASRS Gantry Robots operate on tracks to move horizontally and vertically to retrieve goods from storage locations. Each type of ASRS system offers unique benefits for different storage and retrieval needs.</p></p>
