<p><ul><li>Diaphragm structure</li><li>Pistona structure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Piezoelectric force sensors come in two main types - diaphragm structure and piston structure. Diaphragm structure sensors use a thin pressure-sensitive diaphragm to measure force, making them suitable for applications requiring high levels of accuracy and sensitivity. On the other hand, piston structure sensors utilize a piston that moves under force, making them ideal for applications requiring high dynamic response and durability. Both types offer distinct advantages depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
