<p><p>Thermal Cycling Thermoelectric Modules (TEM) are categorized into Single Stage and Multistage TEM markets. Single Stage TEMs are designed to operate at a single temperature difference, while Multistage TEMs can operate at multiple temperature differences within the same module. Single Stage TEMs are typically used in applications where a small temperature difference needs to be maintained, while Multistage TEMs are used in more complex systems requiring precise temperature control across multiple stages. Both types of TEMs play crucial roles in various industries for thermal management.</p></p>
