<p><p>PTFE Dispersion Market includes two main types: PTFE Oil Dispersion and PTFE Aqueous Dispersion. PTFE Oil Dispersion is a type of dispersion where PTFE particles are dispersed in oil, providing benefits such as lubrication and anti-stick properties. On the other hand, PTFE Aqueous Dispersion involves dispersing PTFE particles in water, leading to applications such as non-stick coatings and waterproofing. Both types offer unique advantages and are widely used in various industries for their distinct properties.</p></p>
