<p><p>Lock cores market can be categorized into two types: uncombinated cores and combinated cores. Uncombinated cores are sold without any key, allowing the user to create their own unique key. On the other hand, combinated cores are sold with a pre-determined key that matches other cores within a set. Both types cater to different needs, with uncombinated cores offering customization and combinated cores providing convenience for users who require multiple locks to be operated with the same key.</p></p>
