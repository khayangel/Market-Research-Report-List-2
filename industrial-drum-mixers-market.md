<p><ul><li>Top Mounted</li><li>Side Mounted</li><li>Bottom Mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial drum mixers are classified into three types based on their mounting position - top mounted, side mounted, and bottom mounted. Top mounted mixers are attached to the top of the drum and are ideal for blending lightweight materials. Side mounted mixers are attached to the side of the drum and are suitable for mixing viscous liquids. Bottom mounted mixers are attached to the bottom of the drum and are commonly used for mixing heavy materials. Each type of mixer offers unique benefits depending on the application requirements.</p></p>
