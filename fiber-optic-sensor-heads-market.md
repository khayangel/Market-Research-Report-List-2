<p><p>Fiber optic sensor heads are classified into two types based on their design and usage: intrinsic and extrinsic. Intrinsic fiber optic sensor heads are designed to interact directly with the medium being measured, providing accurate and real-time data. On the other hand, extrinsic fiber optic sensor heads are used in combination with external components to measure various parameters. Both types have their unique applications and advantages, catering to different industries and requirements in the market.</p></p>
