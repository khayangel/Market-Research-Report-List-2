<p><p>Thermostat market types can be categorized into four main categories: Non-Programmable Type, Programmable Type, Smart Type, and WiFi Type. Non-Programmable thermostats allow for manual temperature adjustments, while Programmable thermostats enable users to set desired temperatures at scheduled times. Smart thermostats connect to the internet and can be controlled remotely through a smartphone app. WiFi thermostats offer similar functionality but specifically rely on WiFi connections to communicate and adjust settings from anywhere. Each type offers different levels of convenience and control for regulating home temperatures.</p></p>
