<p><p>Intraoperative imaging (IOI) system is a technology used during surgical procedures to provide real-time images of the patient's anatomy. There are three main types of IOI systems: Intraoperative CT, Intraoperative MRI, and Intraoperative Ultrasound. Intraoperative CT uses computed tomography to produce detailed cross-sectional images during surgery. Intraoperative MRI provides high-resolution images using magnetic resonance imaging technology. Intraoperative Ultrasound uses sound waves to create images of internal body structures. These technologies aid surgeons in making informed decisions and improving surgical outcomes.</p></p>
