<p><p>The non-threaded fastener market encompasses various types, including screws, nuts, rivets, and others. Screws are used to hold materials together through rotational motion, while nuts secure screws in place. Rivets are permanent fasteners used to join materials by deforming the shaft to form a head. Other non-threaded fasteners include pins, clips, and washers, each serving specific purposes in construction and manufacturing industries. These fasteners play a crucial role in securing and assembling various structures and components.</p></p>
